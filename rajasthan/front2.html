RAJASTHAN




A b o u t


Rajasthan is the largest state of the Republic of India in terms of area . George Thomas first named this province Rajputana in 1800 AD. Famous historian James Tad named this state as Raithan or Rajasthan in "Annals and Antiquities of Rajasthan" . This state has an international border of 1070 km with Pakistan , which is known as the Red Cliff Line, and a 4850 km interstate border which is also connected to five other states of the country. Gujarat in its south-west , Madhya Pradesh in the north, Punjab (India) in the north , Uttar Pradesh and Haryana in the north-eastIs. The area of ​​the state is 3,42,239 square kilometers (132,140 square miles). According to 2011 census, the literacy rate of Rajasthan is 66.1%.

The capital of the state is Jaipur. Geographical features include the Thar Desert in the west and the end of the Ghaggar River. Aravalli range, which is prominent among the ancient ranges of the world, is the only mountain range of Rajasthan, which is a center of tourism, it includes Mount Abu and the world famous Delwara Temple . Rajasthan has three (four after the addition of Ramgarh Vishdhari) tiger reserves, Mukandra Hills , Ranthambore and Sariska and Keoladeo National Park near Bharatpur .Which has been developed as a protected habitat for cranes coming from remote Siberia and a large number of local species of birds. The newest division of Rajasthan is Bharatpur. The smallest district of Rajasthan in terms of area is Dudu , the area of ​​Dholpur is 3034 square km . And the largest district is Jaisalmer. Whose area is 38401 square km. The hottest place in India is Phalodi Jodhpur. A large number of solar power plants are being established in Phalodi. At present 50 districts and 10 divisions have been created in Rajasthan.
